# Guides

Practical, start-to-finish walkthroughs. Each one takes you from nothing to a working
result using only in-game commands.

- **[Getting started](/docs/guides/getting-started)** — your balance, paying players,
  and reading your transaction history.
- **[Using the Explorer & linking your account](/docs/guides/using-the-explorer)** —
  browse the site, sign in, and link your Minecraft account to see your own data.
- **[Setting up a business](/docs/guides/setting-up-a-business)** — register a firm,
  fund it, and run it.
- **[Hiring & managing staff](/docs/guides/hiring-staff)** — offer jobs, build roles,
  and promote or remove staff.
- **[Business accounts & money](/docs/guides/business-accounts)** — fund accounts, split
  money across them, and pay people.
- **[Selling with ChestShops](/docs/guides/chestshop-basics)** — build a chest shop
  with a sign.
- **[ChestShops for your firm](/docs/guides/chestshop-business)** — route shop sales
  into a business account.
- **[Government accounts](/docs/guides/government-accounts)** — manage shared department
  accounts and fines.
- **[Buying & renting property](/docs/guides/property-realty)** — buy, rent, offer, or
  bid on plots.
- **[How tax works](/docs/guides/tax)** — what's taxed, when it's taken, and how to
  keep track.
- **[Government salaries](/docs/guides/salaries)** — automatic pay for official
  government roles.
- **[Using the economy API](/docs/guides/api)** — for developers: issue an API key and
  build against the REST API.
- **[Webhooks](/docs/guides/webhooks)** — get a POST (or a Discord message) whenever your
  account or firm moves money.
