# Getting started with money

You have a bank balance from the moment you first join. This page covers the handful
of commands you'll use every day. Everything here is something you type in chat.

## Check your balance

```text
/bal
```

That shows how much money you have. It also answers to `/balance` and `/money`.

Curious about someone else, or want to see who's doing well?

```text
/baltop
```

`/baltop` lists the richest players, ten at a time — add a page number like
`/baltop 2` to see further down.

## Pay another player

Send money straight to someone:

```text
/pay Steve 250
```

The amount leaves your balance and lands in theirs immediately. If they're online,
they'll get a message letting them know.

> [!TIP]
> You can also pay a **government account** by its name instead of a player — handy
> for paying a department or a fee.

> [!WARNING]
> Double-check the name and amount before you press enter. Payments go through right
> away and can't be reversed by you — you'd have to ask the recipient to send it back.

## See where your money went

Every payment, sale, wage, and fee is recorded. Review your history:

```text
/transactions
```

Each entry shows the amount (in or out), a short note about what it was, and when it
happened. Add a page number to go further back: `/transactions 2`.

Want a copy you can keep or open in a spreadsheet?

```text
/transactions export
```

This hands you a link to a downloadable file of your history.
